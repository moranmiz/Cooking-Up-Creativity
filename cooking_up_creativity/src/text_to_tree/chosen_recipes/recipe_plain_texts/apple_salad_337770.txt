Title: Apple Salad

Ingredients:
8 medium apples (chopped)
1 (8 ounce) jar maraschino cherries (cut in half)
2 stalks celery (chopped)
1 cup red seedless grapes (cut in half)
1 cup coarsely chopped peanuts
34-1 cup Miracle Whip
2 cups Cool Whip
14-12 cup sugar

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients together.
Mix dressing ingredients together well and stir into salad.
Eat immediately as nuts go soft after it sits for a while.
